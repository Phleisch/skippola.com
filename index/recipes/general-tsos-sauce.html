General Tso's Sauce

[link—standalone]

Prep Time

2 minutes

Total Time

5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp hoisin sauce
  • 2 tbsp chili sauce sriracha recommended
  • 2 tbsp sesame oil

Instructions

  1. Mix ingredients in whatever order you desire in a bowl
  2. Use with food. Typically I use this with tofu, rice and stir-fried vegetables
