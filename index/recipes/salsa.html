Prep Time

10 minutes

Total Time

10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 large red onion finely chopped
  • 4 medium tomatoes finely chopped
  • 2 jalape√±o peppers finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves finely chopped
  • 1 lime halved and squeezed
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 tbsp pepper
  • 1/2 cup cilantro

Instructions

  1. Finely chop all fresh ingredients to desired size.
  2. Mix together cut ingredients and spices in a large bowl.
  3. Serve with nachos, tortillas, tacos or whatever your salsa heart desires.
  4. (Optional) Make the recipe your own by adding additional spices, for example cumin, oregano, chili powder, etc.
