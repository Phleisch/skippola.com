General Tso's Sauce

[link—standalone]

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons hoisin sauce
  • 2 teaspoons chili sauce
  • 2 teaspoons sesame oil

Instructions

  1. Mix ingredients in whatever order you desire in a bowl
  2. Use with food. Typically I use this with tofu, rice and stir-fried vegetables
