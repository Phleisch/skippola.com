Chana Masala

[link—standalone]

Ingredients

  • Bowl of chick peas (from can or self-soaked for 12 hours)
  • Olive oil
  • Cumin seeds
  • Medium white onion, diced
  • Medium tomato, diced
  • 2 green peppers, diced
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1/2 tablespoon paprika
  • 1/2 tablespoon turmeric
  • 3/2 tablespoon Chana Masala spice mix
  • 1/2 tablespoon coriander powder

Instructions

  1. Boil chick peas in salted water until soft, then drain and set to the side
  2. Thinly cover pan in olive oil, heat at medium high
  3. Shake cumin seeds over the pan of olive oil, wait for the cumin seeds to change color (but don't burn them)
  4. Add the rest of the ingredients to the pan, plus a small cup of water
  5. Cover lid of pan and let cook for 3 minutes, stir, and the cook for 3 more minutes.
  6. Add chick peas to the pan. Cook for an additional 4-5 minutes
  7. At this point you are done. Serve with warm naan bread or rice, or both (preferred that the rice is long grain Basmati rice)
