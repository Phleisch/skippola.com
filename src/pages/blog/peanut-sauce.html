Peanut Sauce

[link—standalone]

Prep Time

2 minutes

Total Time

5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup peanut butter
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 3 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp toasted sesame oil
  • 2 garlic cloves minced
  • 2 tbsp water

Instructions

  1. Mix together all the ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Taste the sauce. Amend the sauce to your preference. If the sauce is too thick, add more water. If the sauce is too watery, add more peanut butter.
by Kai Fleischman
